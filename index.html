My Personal Interests

One of my biggest interests is writing. Since a pretty young age I've been an aspiring author, and I've outlined and started writing lots of projects over the years. I haven't finished quite so many, but I have finished a few. As a child I was obsessed with Harry Potter, but getting into Brandon Sanderson's works as a teenager was what really inspired me to write my own stories. I think that stories are a fascinating way to explore ideas, and I hope one day to be able to share my own ideas with the world.

Another one of my interests is drumming. Since I got into percussion in middle school it's been a big part of my life, but it wasn't until high school, and marching band, that I really started to get into it. I love the process of perfecting a piece of music, and the feeling of playing with a group of people. I also love the feeling of performing, and the adrenaline rush that comes with it. I now drum in UVU's Green Man Group, and aspire to join a drum corps next summer.

A third interest of mine is anime. Although as a child I mostly consumed stories through books, recently I've found anime to be a great way to experience stories. As I've gotten busier with school and work, anime is a great way to watch a story while also being able to do other things, like drum practice or homework. Additionally, it doubles as practice for my Japanese, which is another interest of mine; insights into another culture are always interesting. Aside from those factors, I just really enjoy the art style and the way stories are told in anime. Any medium will inevitably be hit or miss, but I feel that every anime really commits to its identity, so when it hits it really hits.
